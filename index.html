Tier Structure (Applied to Net Profit USD equivalent)

Internal logic standardizes to USD based on the $1 = NT$33 ratio.

  • NT$0 - NT$50,000 : 5%
  • NT$50,001 - NT$200,000 : 10%
  • NT$200,001 - NT$500,000 : 15%
  • > NT$500,000 : 20%

Campaign Modifiers (Applied to Gross Profit)

  • Normal: 1.0x
